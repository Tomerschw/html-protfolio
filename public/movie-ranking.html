My top 3

Naruto

Naruto is a Japanese anime series based on the manga of the same name by Masashi Kishimoto. It tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. The series is known for its complex characters, intricate plotlines, and intense action scenes. Naruto has been praised for its character development, particularly the growth of Naruto himself from a mischievous and lonely child to a powerful and respected ninja. The series also explores themes of friendship, perseverance, and the importance of never giving up on one's dreams. The anime has a large and dedicated fanbase, and has spawned numerous spin-offs, movies , and video games. It has also been credited with popularizing anime in the West and inspiring a new generation of fans. Overall, Naruto is a beloved and influential anime series that has left a lasting impact on the world of anime and pop culture.

Attack on titan

attack on titan is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. The story is set in a world where humanity is on the brink of extinction due to giant humanoid creatures known as Titans, who devour humans seemingly without reason. The series follows Eren Yeager, his adoptive sister Mikasa Ackerman, and their friend Armin Arlert as they join the military to fight against the Titans and uncover the mysteries surrounding their existence. Attack on Titan has been praised for its intense action scenes, complex characters, and thought-provoking themes. The series explores themes of survival, freedom, and the consequences of war, as well as the nature of humanity and the struggle for power. The manga has been adapted into an anime series, which has also been highly acclaimed and has gained a large following worldwide. The anime features stunning animation, a gripping storyline, and a memorable soundtrack. Overall, Attack on Titan is a thrilling and thought-provoking series that has captivated audiences with its unique premise and compelling characters
